- Stoneware (10 plant pots and 5 coffee cups)

- Programme (7) = [Normal speed (mid)]({{< ref "/blog/2021/stoneware-glaze-firings/index.md#-normal-speed-mid" >}}) but with **20 min soak instead of 40 mins**
