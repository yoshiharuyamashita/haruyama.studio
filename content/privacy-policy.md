Haruyama Studio respects your privacy and is committed to protecting
your personal data.

This policy explains how personal information is collected and used
when you visit this website or get in touch with the studio.

## Information collected

Depending on how you interact with the website, the following
information may be collected:

- your name and email address when you use the contact form  
- messages and information you choose to send via the website  
- basic usage data collected through website hosting or analytics services  

No purchasing or payment processing takes place on this website.

## How information is used

Personal information is used only where necessary, including to:

- respond to enquiries  
- communicate about the studio’s work  
- operate and improve the website  
- meet legal or administrative obligations  

Your information is not sold or shared for marketing purposes.

## Third-party services

This website uses privacy‑respecting analytics provided by
<a href="https://www.goatcounter.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GoatCounter</a>.

GoatCounter does not use tracking cookies or collect personal data.
It provides anonymised information about page visits to help
understand how the website is used.

This website does not use advertising trackers or third‑party
marketing analytics.

## Data retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil
its purpose or to comply with legal obligations.

## Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

- request access to personal data held about you  
- request correction or deletion of your data  
- object to or restrict certain processing  

To make a request, please get in touch using the contact page.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is
handled, please contact the studio via the website.
