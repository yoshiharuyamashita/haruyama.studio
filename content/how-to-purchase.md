Ceramic objects and prints are created and fulfilled
through different processes. For clarity, purchasing,
delivery, and returns are handled separately for each.

The information below will help you find the correct
details depending on the type of work you are interested in.

## Ceramics

Ceramic objects are handmade in small batches in Wales
and sold directly through the ceramics shop.

Delivery, returns, and care information for ceramic pieces
are outlined within the ceramics shop itself.

## Prints

Prints are produced to order and fulfilled through
the prints shop.

Delivery timelines, returns, and terms for prints are
provided within the prints shop and may differ from
ceramic objects.
