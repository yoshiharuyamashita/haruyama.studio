Ceramic objects and prints are created and fulfilled
through different processes. For clarity, ordering, delivery and
returns are handled separately for each.

The information below will help you find the correct details
depending on the type of work you are interested in.

## Ceramics

Ceramic objects are handmade in small batches in Wales and sold
directly through the ceramics shop.

Delivery, returns and care information for ceramic pieces are
outlined within the ceramics shop itself.

[View ceramics shop information](https://haruyama-studio.square.site/)

## Prints

Prints are produced to order and fulfilled through
the prints shop.

Delivery timelines, returns and terms for prints are provided
within the prints shop and may differ from ceramic objects.

[View prints shop information](https://prints-haruyama-studio.square.site/)

## Questions

If you have any questions that aren’t covered, or if you’re unsure
which information applies to your order, feel free to get in touch.

[Contact the studio](/contact/)
